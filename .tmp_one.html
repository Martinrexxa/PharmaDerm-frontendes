Product Details

Our hydrating cleanser was developed as a hydrating face wash for those looking for a gentle yet thorough cleanse that won’t leave skin feeling tight or dry. In fact, our unique blend of three essential ceramides and hyaluronic acid also helps nourish and hydrate skin while helping to restore and maintain its essential natural moisture barrier. To help skin reap all the benefits that this hydrating cleanser provides, our MVE Delivery Technology encapsulates ceramides to ensure efficient delivery within the skin’s barrier and slowly releases them over time—supporting it long after you’ve rinsed this face wash for dry skin away. It is dermatologist-recommended to help repair the skin barrier.

How to Use

How to Steps

  • 1
    Wet skin with lukewarm water
  • 2
    Lather this hydrating cleanser between your palms, then massage it onto your face in gentle, circular motions.
  • 3
    Rinse well, and gently pat your skin dry.
  • 4

    Follow this hydrating face wash with a hydrating moisturizer, such as CeraVe Moisturizing Cream (or for dry to very dry skin, give CeraVe Intensive Moisturizing Cream a try).

When

    Time of Day:
    Anytime
